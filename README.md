## 📌 Overview

SwasthyaAI is a smart AI-based healthcare mobile application designed to support people in rural and low-resource areas where access to hospitals, doctors, and healthcare awareness is limited.

The app helps users identify symptoms through voice or text, detect emergency severity, locate nearby hospitals, scan prescriptions, manage medicine schedules, and receive healthcare guidance even in low or no internet connectivity.

By combining **Artificial Intelligence, Voice Assistance, OCR, Offline Access, and Multilingual Support**, SwasthyaAI aims to bridge the healthcare gap for millions of people across villages and underserved regions.

# ✨ Features

## 🤖 AI Voice Symptom Analysis
Users can describe symptoms using voice in local languages.

Example:
> “Mujhe bukhar aur khansi hai”

AI analyzes:
- Possible health concern
- Basic advice
- Risk severity
- Doctor recommendation

---

## 🚨 Emergency Risk Detection
Traffic-light triage model:

🔴 **Red** → Immediate medical help needed  
🟡 **Yellow** → Monitor closely  
🟢 **Green** → Safe/basic care

---

## 🏥 Nearby Hospital & PHC Locator
Find:
- Government hospitals
- PHC (Primary Health Centers)
- Clinics
- Ambulance support
- Nearby emergency services

## 📄 Prescription OCR Scanner

# 🧠 Problem Statement

Millions of people in rural India face delayed treatment because of:
- Limited hospitals
- Lack of doctors
- Language barriers
- Poor healthcare awareness
- Weak internet connectivity
- Low digital literacy

SwasthyaAI solves this by delivering **AI-driven healthcare support directly to users in a simple mobile app.**
